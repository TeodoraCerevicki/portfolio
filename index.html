About Thea

Hello there! It's a pleasure to connect with you.
I'm Teodora, but you can call me Thea.
I've delved into the realm of IT since 2017, navigating through diverse projects, teams, and dynamic environments. Initially, my focus was on elevating web aesthetics using the SASS extension language. However, my recent journey has involved a deeper dive into JavaScript and the fascinating realms of UX/UI.

My academic journey led me to a bachelor's degree in Web Design from the Higher Technical School of Applied Studies in Novi Sad. If you're curious about my professional ventures, you'll find more details on my LinkedIn profile.

When it comes to coding, I'm a firm believer in keeping things nice and clean. Organized code isn't just visually appealing—it's the backbone that simplifies updating existing elements and seamlessly integrating new features. It's all about setting a strict standard right from the start. After all, a disciplined approach to structure and cleanliness is the key to an efficient and hassle-free coding journey, wouldn't you say? :)

As for my professional experience, I've detailed my projects and work in the “Experience” section, as well as links to some of the projects on LinkedIn, saving the nitty-gritty for there.

Outside the coding realm, my free time revolves around my furry companion — I love spending quality moments cuddling, playing in the dog park, and meeting friends. I'm an avid reader, breaking my own record in 2023 by devouring 18 novels. Theater holds a special place in my heart, and I find joy in attending compelling plays.

I consider myself quite crafty, with hobbies ranging from playing the cello to sewing, crocheting, and even exploring stripping and weaving, although time only sometimes permits these passions. Here's to hoping I'll carve out more time for these creative pursuits.
